About the School

At Habla Colombia our team of experienced Colombian teachers will teach you Spanish through interactive, immersive and fun one-on-one classes. Whether you are a beginner or already an advanced learner, from lesson one you will be learning new vocabulary and grammar.


The school is run by the teachers, giving your teacher freedom to design your classes in the way they feel best suits your needs. This also means 100% of your tuition fee goes directly to your teacher!
