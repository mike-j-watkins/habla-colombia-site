About our school

open new doors

Spanish: the language of Latin America, Spain and even Equatorial Guinea! With speakers all over the world, Spanish is spoken by millions globally. Based in Medellín, Colombia with its famously neutral accent, Habla Colombia is perfect for learning Spanish that will be understood anywhere!

The Habla Colombia method

Here at Habla Colombia we specialize in personalized, one-to-one Spanish lessons. Whether you are a student, traveller, looking to expand your business opportunities or just learning to broaden your horizons, our lessons can be tailored to your needs. Our teaching method focuses on student communication, so from day one you will be speaking real-world Spanish!

 

About our teachers

Fully qualified

All of our teachers are native Spanish speakers with teaching degrees and have at least 2 years experience teaching Spanish as a second language. We focus on covering the 4 language areas of reading, writing, speaking and listening with a focus on speaking and listening for effective real-world communication.

Highly motivated

Our teachers are highly enthusiastic, designing lessons around topics they feel you will have fun exploring together. Many of our students choose Colombia to visit based on the warm, fun personalities of our teachers! Unlike other schools, the school is teacher owned; there are no middlemen or managers, so your teacher will receive 100% of your tuition fee and is personally invested in your success and enjoyment.

Explore a new culture

Learn about Latin America, Colombia and Medellín. Music, people, food and stereotypes (true and untrue!)

Find your way

Learn to navigate the world in Spanish. Eating out, shopping, getting about and getting to know people!

See Medellín

Visit monuments, markets and places of interest with our in-person lessons (available as covid restrictions allow)

Make new friends

Teachers often stay in touch with students past the end of their courses, help out if you're stuck abroad and meet up if you're passing through Medellín!
